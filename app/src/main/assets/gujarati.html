GUJARATI (ગુજરાતી)


Information -

Gujarati is a western Indo-Aryan language spoken predominantly in the state of Gujarat which is located to the west of India. The language is more than 700 years old. 

 

 

 

The script –

QUICK FACT – If you know the Devanagari script (Hindi, Marathi, Sanskrit) then you already know more than 70% of the symbols used in the gujarati script.

 

The Gujarati alphabet has 13 vowels or સ્વર (swar, स्वर) and 36 consonants or વ્યંજન (vyanjan, व्यंजन). The consonants produce an incomplete sound on their own and usually join with a vowel to take 13 forms (1 form for each vowel).
