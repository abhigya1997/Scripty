PUNJABI (ਪੰਜਾਬੀ)

Brief History –

Punjabi is a North-western Indo-Aryan language spoken predominantly in the state of Punjab.

 

 

The script –

QUICK FACT – Few characters in this language can be quite misleading as they resemble some Devanagari characters with a different sound.
